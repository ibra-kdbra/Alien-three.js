# Product

## Register

brand

## Users
Gamers, exploration enthusiasts, and sci-fi fans seeking an immersive, atmospheric, and mechanically satisfying 3D survival game.

## Product Purpose
Provide a highly polished, retro-futuristic planetary reconnaissance simulation. Players explore a procedurally generated planet to locate signal beacons before running out of oxygen. Success is measured by seamless controls, a tense atmosphere, and premium visual fidelity.

## Brand Personality
- **High-Fidelity Retro-Futurism**: Glitchy, low-tech CRT monitor overlays mixed with modern PBR lighting and post-processing effects.
- **Tense Isolation**: Atmospheric hums, eerie toxic vents, and a ticking oxygen bar that pulses as danger approaches.
- **Tactile Weight**: A control scheme that respects momentum and gravity on a spherical world.

## Anti-references
- **Hyper-bright Arcade Style**: Flat, neon-drenched levels that feel gamey instead of cinematic and atmospheric.
- **Floaty Physics**: Floaty movement where the player slides endlessly or clips through hills.
- **Generic SaaS aesthetics**: Avoid clean, sterile white panels and flat icons. The design should remain a rugged tactile terminal interface.

## Design Principles
1. **Spherical Cohesion**: Everything—physics colliders, gravity, camera rigs, and visual assets—must conform naturally to the planet's spherical normal.
2. **Tactile Retro Interface**: The suit's HUD behaves like a physical retro CRT monitor built into the helmet.
3. **Environmental Hostility**: Shaders, fog, and particle systems combine to represent a hostile, toxic planet surface.

## Accessibility & Inclusion
- **High-Contrast Indicators**: Vital telemetry (Oxygen, Signal) uses distinct color progression (Green → Orange → Flashing Red) with accompanying clear numerical indicators.
- **Reduced Motion Support**: Provide a way to disable heavy camera shakes or HUD glitch animations if desired.
- **Readability**: High-legibility monospaced font for logs and tactical readout telemetry.
